Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak German to study in Germany? expand_more
While many undergraduate programs are taught in German, there is a growing number of English-taught programs, especially at the Master's level. However, learning basic German is highly recommended for daily life.
Are universities really free in Germany? expand_more
Yes! Public universities in 15 out of 16 German states charge no tuition fees for undergraduate students, regardless of nationality. You only pay a semester contribution of around €150-€350.
Can I work while studying? expand_more
Yes, international students from outside the EU are allowed to work 120 full days or 240 half days per year.
