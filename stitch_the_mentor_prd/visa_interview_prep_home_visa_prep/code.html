Required Documents Checklist

check_circle

Passport & Photos

Valid for at least 6 months beyond your period of stay. 2 recent color photos.

description

Admission Letter (I-20 / CAS)

Original acceptance letter from the university. Check for accurate program dates.

account_balance

Financial Proof

Bank statements, scholarship letters, or loan sanction letters covering tuition + living.

school

Academic Transcripts

Original mark sheets, provisional certificates, and standardized test scores (GRE/TOEFL).

Common Interview Questions

Study Plans expand_circle_down

Why did you choose this specific university?

Focus on the curriculum, professors, and how it aligns with your career goals. Avoid generic answers about rankings.

Finances expand_circle_down

Who is sponsoring your education?

Be clear about the relationship with your sponsor and their income source. Bring proof of liquid assets.

Future Plans expand_circle_down

What are your plans after graduation?

Crucial: Demonstrate intent to return home. Discuss career prospects in your home country.
